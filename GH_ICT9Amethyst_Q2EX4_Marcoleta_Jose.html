Which Reinhardt Skins Should You Buy?

Balderich


>This skin was made in honor of Reinhardt's old friend, Balderich. This skin is Reinhardt using Balderich's armor with many gold accents, which makes it a fantastic skin. This skin is worth 1900 Overwatch Coins or equivalent to 20 US Dollars.















Minotaur

>This is the recently released Reinhardt skin in the Battle for Olympus. Which has a theme of Greek mythology, hence the name Battle for Olympus. This skin is based of a mythological creature, the minotaur. It clearly grasps the menacing look of the minotaur, which makes it a really good skin for Reinhardt. This skin costs 1900 Overwatch Coins as well.
